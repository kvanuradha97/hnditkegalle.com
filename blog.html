Higher National Diploma (HND) Programs at SLIATE

The Higher National Diploma (HND) programs offered by the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE) provide advanced education and practical skills to students who have successfully completed their GCE (A/L) examinations.

Programs and Specializations

  • Accountancy (HNDA): Prepares students for careers in auditing and accounting.
  • Agriculture (HNDT Agri): Focuses on agricultural practices and management.
  • Business Administration (HNDBA): Equips students with business management skills.
  • Engineering (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical): Provides specialized engineering knowledge.
  • Information Technology (HNDIT): Covers IT concepts, programming, and systems.
  • Quantity Surveying (HNDQS): Focuses on construction cost estimation.
  • Tourism and Hospitality Management (HNDTHM): Prepares students for the hospitality industry.

Admission and Eligibility

Students who have completed their GCE (A/L) exams are eligible to apply for HND courses at SLIATE. The application process is typically done online via the SLIATE website.

Career Prospects

HND holders can pursue various career paths, such as becoming registered auditors, working in management roles, or contributing to engineering projects. These programs emphasize practical skills, making graduates well-prepared for the workforce.
